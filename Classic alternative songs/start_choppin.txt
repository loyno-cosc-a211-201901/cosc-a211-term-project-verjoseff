"Start Choppin" is a song by American rock band Dinosaur Jr.
It was written by frontman J Mascis and featured on their 1993 album "Where You Been".
The song title came from portions of different recorded versions of the song being combined into the final version.
"Start Choppin" is known for Mascis's catchy guitar work.
Pitchfork ranked it number 93 in their "Top 200 Tracks of the 1990s" list.
The song's music video became an alternative rock music program staple.
