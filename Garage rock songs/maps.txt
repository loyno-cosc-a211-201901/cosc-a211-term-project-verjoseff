"Maps" is a song from the American indie rock band Yeah Yeah Yeahs.
It is a single from their debut album "Fever to Tell" (2004).
The song is about the relationship between the band's frontwoman Karen O and Liars frontman Angus Andrew.
In fact, the song title allegedly stands for "My Angus Please Stay".
"Maps" is notable for its music video, which features Karen O's unscripted crying.
The song is one of the band's most popular songs.
