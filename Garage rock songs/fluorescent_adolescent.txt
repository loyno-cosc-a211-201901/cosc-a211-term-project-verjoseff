"Fluorescent Adolescent" is a song by the English indie rock band Arctic Monkeys.
It was released as the second single from their second studio album "Favourite Worst Nightmare" (2007).
The song was written by the band's lead singer Alex Turner and his then-girlfriend Johanna Bennett.
The song is about nostalgia for youth, from the perspective of a former prostitute.
"Fluorescent Adolescent" is one of the band's most popular songs, peaking at number 5 in the UK.
The music video was directed by "The IT Crowd"'s Richard Ayoade and features the band dressed as clowns.
