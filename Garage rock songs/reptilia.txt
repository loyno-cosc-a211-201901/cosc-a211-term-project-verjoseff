"Reptilia" is a song by indie rock band The Strokes.
It was written by the band's lead vocalist Julian Casablancas.
It was released as the second single from their second studio album "Room on Fire" (2003).
It is one of the band's most popular songs.
The song's title refers to a scientific term for the human brain's higher-level base instincts and emotions.
The song is basically about the singer acting on impulse rather than reason.
