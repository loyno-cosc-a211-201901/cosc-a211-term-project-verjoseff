"Wonderwall" is a song by the English rock band Oasis.
It was written by the band's lead guitarist and main songwriter Noel Gallagher.
It was produced by Owen Morris and Gallagher.
"Wonderwall" was released as the fourth single from the band's second studio album "(What's the Story) Morning Glory?" (1995).
The song is about "an imaginary friend who's gonna come and save you from yourself," according to Gallagher.
It is the one of the band's most popular songs, reaching number two in the UK and number eight in the US.
